So dead

Aloe Vera

More like Byebye Vera...

I got a happy, healthy aloe from my bud Leslie who moved to SF like a start up cliche. Needless to say, it's super dead now.

A brief list of my crimes:

  1. Repotted healthy aloe into a bigger pot with potting soil instead of cactus soil!!
  2. Rerepotted with cactus soil but became convinced it was suffering root rot and unpotted once again, destroyed most of the roots, and then rererepotted
  3. Became convinced that larger leaves were dead and that it would regrow its roots better with fewer leaves so I cut off all its big leaves
  4. Finally, left it in an unbelievably afternoon hot sun windowsill

Now it is yellow and shrivelled and definitely dead. Sorry Leslie!!

On the fritz

Boston Fern

For whom this is named

Another Leslie adoption, the fern was in questionable condition from the begining.

A brief list of my crimes:

  1. Repotted into white plastic hanging container with no drainage holes
  2. Panicked and rerepotted into plastic baby blue pot with drainage holes
  3. Moved pot from room to room almost daily to try to see some kind of change

Road to salvation:

  • Housed in bright indirect light in the bathroom
  • Watered twice a week with a few drops of plant food mmmmyum
  • Whispered sweet nothings
  • Provided physical and emotional space for growth

And honestly, I think BF is gonna make it!

Two green thumbs up

Spider Plant

My mom reminds me that spider plants are indestructable

In May, my bud Sofia hosted a New Moon party so that we could drink some Kombucha and plant some clippings from her Spider Plant.

This is the story of one such clipping:

  1. Filled a small water glass halfway with pebbles and marbles, the other half with potting soil, and the lil guy nestled inside
  2. Watered the darling whenever the soil was dry
  3. Eventually repotted into an Ikea ceramic pot with potting soil and pebbles at the bottom for drainage
  4. Looked at it lovingly and encouragingly

Sometimes I pick its pot up and walk around saying "Look how happy it is!"
